More Information on LaGuardia

The LaGuardia Dance Department is very well known all over the world. We have alumni such as Genessy Castillo, Gregory Lau ands Waverly Fredricks who have all taken their training and time from LaGuardia High School with them into their professional lives and have booked jobs like Broadway, Netherlands Dance Theater, The Super Bowl, filming music videos with artists like Arianna Grane and so much more. LaGuardia Dance is truly the place to be and will change the landscape and playing field of your life forever.
