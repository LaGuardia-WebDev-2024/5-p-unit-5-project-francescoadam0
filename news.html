Breif Overview on the class of 2025

The graduating senior class of 2025 SENIOR DANCE MAJORS have been working and training hard every single day, 5 days a week for the past 4 years. Through non stop rehersals and extra time spent outside of school, we put in all the hard work and dedication needed to present such an amazing show. Over the 4 years we have been training in all styles including Ballet, Jazz, Horton Tecnique, Limon Technique, Graham Technique, Tap and we even studied Choreography in our 2nd semester of junior year and presented our work in the 2024 Junior Projects Showcase. Our freshman year we studied Survivial Skills, our sophomore year we studied Dance History and Dance Anatomy and out junior year we studied theater dance history and even took vocl for a semester. The work that has been put in by both the dance department faculty and the senior dance majors here at LaGuardia is extraordinary The faculty is the guiding tool that us students need in order tofaculty is the guiding tool that us students need in order to be sucessful in whatever path and route we decide to go on. Without their knowledge, effort, commitement and decication, we would not have the abilities and strengh that we have today.
The Dance Department provides a rigorous conservatory style training program to build a strong foundation and to support current demands for versatility. We commit to making diversity and inclusion a key component in our training to foster an equitable learning environment for all to thrive.

LaGuardia Alumni and History

The LaGuardia Dance Department is very well known all over the world. We have alumni such as Genessy Castillo, Gregory Lau ands Waverly Fredricks who have all taken their training and time from LaGuardia High School with them into their professional lives and have booked jobs like Broadway, Netherlands Dance Theater, The Super Bowl, filming music videos with artists like Arianna Grane and so much more. LaGuardia Dance is truly the place to be and will change the landscape and playing field of your life forever.

The LaGuardia Dance Class of 2025 is constantly pushing themselves everyday above and beyond their standards working extra hard to put on an amazing Winter Dance Showcase which we have been waiting almost 4 years for.
