Breif Overview on the class of 2025

The graduating senior class of 2025 SENIOR DANCE MAJORS have been working and training hard every single day, 5 days a week for the past 4 years. Through non stop rehersals and extra time spent outside of school, we put in all the hard work and dedication needed to present such an amazing show. Over the 4 years we have been training in all styles including Ballet, Jazz, Horton Tecnique, Limon Technique, Graham Technique, Tap and we even studied Choreography in our 2nd semester of junior year and presented our work in the 2024 Junior Projects Showcase. The work that has been put in by both the dance department faculty and the senior dance majors here at LaGuardia is extraordinary The faculty is the guiding tool that us students need in order tofaculty is the guiding tool that us students need in order to be sucessful in whatever path and route we decide to go on. Without their knowledge, effort, commitement and decication, we would not have the abilities and strengh that we have today.
The Dance Department provides a rigorous conservatory style training program to build a strong foundation and to support current demands for versatility. We commit to making diversity and inclusion a key component in our training to foster an equitable learning environment for all to thrive.
